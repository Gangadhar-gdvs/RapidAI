MediBill

Your Pharmacy’s Digital Assistant

Modern, cloud-based billing & inventory software for pharmacies and medical distributors.

Key Features:
  • Fast Billing System – Handle walk-ins & online orders with ease
  • Inventory Management – Track stock, expiry, low-stock alerts
  • Sales Reports – Daily, monthly, and GST reports in one click
  • Cloud Backup – Never lose your data, stored securely in the cloud
  • Multi-user Access – Staff & admin access with role controls
